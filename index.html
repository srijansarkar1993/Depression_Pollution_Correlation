What is it and who is it for?

The visualization shows the correlation between air pollution and depression ratio across a continent. This visualization is targeted for anyone interested in this topic.

Why is it important?

The relationship between air pollution and mental health is a growing area of concern and could have significant implications for public health. Understanding the relationship between air pollution and depression is important for improving public health, reducing the burden of mental illness, and ultimately improving quality of life for individuals and communities affected by air pollution.

How do you look at it?

The top part visualize the severity of air pollution, while the bottom part visualize the severity if depression in a particular country.The position of the red dots visualize the correlation between air pollution and depression.
